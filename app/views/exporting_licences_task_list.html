Part of: Exporting

Export licences and special rules

You must have a licence, or follow special rules to export some things.

Check if you need a licence or to follow special rules:

  • Agricultural machinery
  • Alcohol, fuel and tobacco
  • Animal products and by-products
  • Animal semen, ova and embryos
  • Art works, antiques and culturally significant goods
  • Chemicals
  • Drugs and medicines
  • Food and agricultural products
  • Food for animals
  • Diamonds, gold and precious metals
  • Goods that could be used for restraint, repression or inhumane treatment
  • Horses and ponies
  • Live animals
  • Live fish and shellfish
  • Livestock and poultry
  • Luxury goods to North Korea and Syria
  • Medical devices
  • Military or dual use goods, services and technology
  • Oil and gas related goods and services to Russia
  • Ozone depleting substances and F-gases
  • Plants, seeds, bulbs and wood
  • Get a certificate to export products made from endangered animals
  • Radioactive substances
  • Waste
