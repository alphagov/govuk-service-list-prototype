Exporting and doing business abroad

  1. Get export advice and finance

  2. Export licences and special rules

    3. You must have a licence, or follow special rules to export some things.

    • Check if you need a licence or to follow special rules to export
  3. Register for an EORI number

    4. You must register for an EORI number, if you don’t have one. How you apply depends on whether you’re:

    • registered for VAT
    • not VAT-registered and you’re exporting
    • not VAT-registered and you’re importing
  4. Commodity codes and reporting
