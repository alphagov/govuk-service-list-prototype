Get help paying for childcare

There are various options to get help paying for childcare depending on your situation.

Guidance

Help paying for childcare

  • Check if your are using eligible childcare

    • You must be using "approved childcare" to get help. Check if a childcare provider is approved or search for one in:

    • England
    • Wales
    • Scotland
    • Northern Ireland
  • Get free childcare

    • All 3 and 4-year-olds in England are entitled to 570 hours of free early education or childcare a year. This is often taken as 15 hours each week for 38 weeks of the year.

    Some 2-year-olds are also eligible.

    • Find free early education and childcare
  • Get help with childcare if you are working

    • Check what help you could get with childcare costs
    • Tax credits

      • If you are eligible for tax credits you can get an extra amount for help with childcare.

    • Universal credit

      • If you are eligible for universal credit you can get an extra amount for help with childcare.

    • Childcare vouchers: better off calculator

      • If you are offered vouchers through your employer. Childcare vouchers may affect the amount of tax credits you get.

  • Get help with childcare if you're a student

    • If you are a student with children you may be eligible for one of the following:

    • Discretionary learner support

      • If you are 20 or over and in a further education course

    • Childcare grant

      • If you are in full time higher education in England

    • Care to learn

      • If you are under 20 and in school, 6th form or college
